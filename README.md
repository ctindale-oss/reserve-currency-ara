# 🧩 reserve-currency-ara

**Agent-Native Research Artifact (ARA) v1.0**  
by Craig Tindale (@ctindale)

This is the full conversion of my X essay "How currency strength redirects capital, weakens industrial capacity, and reduces policy effectiveness over time" into the **Agent-Native** format proposed in "The Last Human-Written Paper" (Liu et al., arXiv:2604.24658).

**Why ARA?**  
No more storytelling tax or engineering tax — the full branching thinking, code, evidence, and exploration are preserved and agent-executable.

**Try it right now**  
Copy any folder into Grok and say: "You are inside ctindale's ARA. Explain the mechanism..."

**Structure**
- `/logic` – Scientific logic + causal graph
- `/code` – Executable models
- `/exploration` – Full thinking process + dead ends
- `/evidence` – Grounded sources
- `/template` – One-click template for your next essay

Original human essay: https://x.com/ctindale/status/2041325114684571843

Made as the first Essay-Native ARA example. Star ⭐ if you're excited about the future of writing!
